Who We Are

Qisher Ltd is a leading management consultancy firm in Kenya with clientele base in public, private and NGO organizations. Our scope of services include training and consultancy in Business Strategy, Quality Management Systems, Environmental Management Systems, Information Security Management Systems, Occupational Health And Safety Management Systems, Food Safety Management Systems, Laboratory Management Systems, Conformity Assessment, Energy Management Systems and Lead Auditors Courses.

We also offer Lean management systems including Kaizen , just in time and Kanban. We are registered with National Quality Institute (NQI), National Industrial Training Authority (NITA), National Construction Authority (NCA) and Women Access to Government procurement Services (WAGPO) to ensure we operate within the scope of national regulatory framework. Our experts have vast experience in the areas having worked for Kenya Bureau of Standards, Kenya Accreditation Service (KENAS) among others.
