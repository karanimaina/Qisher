Why Choose Us

Qisher Ltd is a leading management consultancy firm in Kenya with clientele base in public, private and NGO organizations. Our scope of services include training and consultancy in Business Strategy, Quality Management Systems, Environmental Management Systems, Information Security Management Systems, Occupational Health And Safety Management Systems, Food Safety Management Systems, Laboratory Management Systems, Conformity Assessment, Energy Management Systems and Lead Auditors Courses. We also offer Lean management systems includingKaizen , just in time and Kanban. We are registered with National Quality Institute (NQI), National Industrial Training Authority (NITA), National Construction Authority (NCA) and Women Access to Government procurement Services (WAGPO) to ensure we operate within the scope of national regulatory framework. Our experts have vast experience in the areas having worked for Kenya Bureau of Standards, Kenya Accreditation Service (KENAS) among others.

Mission

To provide expert advice and support to organizations in improving their products, processes, and systems in order to meet quality standards and customer requirements.

We offer a range of services, including quality management consulting, quality assurance and control, process improvement, and training and development. Our ultimate goal is to help the organization achieve and maintain a high level of quality in its operations and deliverables.

Vision

To be a trusted advisor and partner for organizations

We enable organizations achieve and sustain excellence in quality. We strive to be recognized as a leader in the quality consulting industry, offering innovative solutions and best practices to meet the evolving needs of their clients. Our firm aims to create a positive impact on our clients' businesses, enabling them to achieve their goals and succeed in their respective markets. Ultimately, our vision is to promote and advance the principles of quality management and continuous improvement globally.
